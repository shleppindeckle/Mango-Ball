About the Fundraiser

We are hard at work getting ready for Mango Ball #5. This year we are going to raffle off pieces of art donated by our favorite Miami artists to raise funds to help cover the costs (paying the DJs, fizzy water, venue costs, etc).

Purchase raffle tickets for a chance to win this featured artwork.
Each ticket is one entry into the drawing. Grab as many tickets as you'd like to increase your chances (and support the Mango Ball!).

You will receive your raffle number via email before the drawing. On the day of the drawing the winning number will be posted online and the winner will be contacted via email.
